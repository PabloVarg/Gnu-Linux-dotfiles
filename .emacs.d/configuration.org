** Visit configuration

#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
  (defun visit-emacs-config ()
    (interactive)
    (find-file "~/.emacs.d/configuration.org"))

  (global-set-key (kbd "C-c e") 'visit-emacs-config)
#+END_SRC
